Why TourBuddy

Tour schedules are messy. Friend logistics are worse.

When everyone is moving city to city, it is hard to know whether two tours overlap in the same place on the same day. TourBuddy pulls that answer forward so you can spend less time cross-checking posters and more time actually seeing people.

Features

Schedule matching

View your own tour dates and compare them against another artist, crew member, or friend in one place.

Poster or website upload

Drop in a friend's tour poster or link their show dates page to check whether your routes overlap in the same city or somewhere nearby.

Timezone-aware sleep planning

Shift your sleep schedule gradually as your tour moves so your body can keep up with changing time zones.
