Privacy Policy

How TourBuddy collects, uses, and stores your information.

TourBuddy provides tools for importing tour dates, planning sleep schedules across time zones, and checking routing overlaps. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices available to you.

Last updated: April 20, 2026

Information We Collect

Information you provide directly

When you create or use a TourBuddy account, you may provide:

  • your email address
  • your display name
  • your artist or band name
  • your home city
  • your sleep preferences, including bedtime, wake time, home time zone, and selected calendar choice

When you use TourBuddy features, you may also provide:

  • tour names
  • tour stops, including dates, cities, venues, countries, and routing details
  • match reminder notes
  • poster uploads or screenshots used for tour extraction
  • edits and review feedback you make while correcting imported poster results

Information created through your use of the app

We may store information generated through the app, including:

  • saved tours and tour stops
  • sleep overrides for specific dates
  • match reminders
  • poster import debug or extraction data needed to support poster review and import quality

Calendar and photo library access

TourBuddy requests access only when you choose to use those features:

  • Photo library access is used so you can select tour posters or screenshots for import.
  • Calendar access is used so you can import calendar events or export sleep blocks and match reminders.

We do not request camera or microphone access in the current app flow.

How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • create and manage your account
  • save your tours and preferences
  • import and normalize tour dates
  • calculate sleep windows and schedule overrides
  • compare tours for same-city overlaps
  • help you export events using calendar integrations
  • troubleshoot and improve poster import reliability
  • provide customer support and respond to requests

How Information Is Stored

TourBuddy currently uses Supabase-backed storage for account and app data. Poster extraction may also involve requests to a hosted OCR or parsing backend configured by the app operator.

Because the app stores account-linked tour data and poster import review data, some information you submit may remain stored until you delete it or request deletion, subject to operational retention needs.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share data with service providers that help us operate the app, such as:

  • authentication and hosted database providers
  • backend infrastructure used for poster extraction and route processing
  • platform services required to export calendar events

We share only the information needed to provide those services.

Tracking and Advertising

TourBuddy is not designed as an advertising product, and the current app flow does not include third-party advertising or cross-app tracking features.

If analytics, advertising, or tracking are added later, this Privacy Policy should be updated before those features are released.

Your Choices

You can:

  • edit your profile and preferences in the app
  • delete saved or archived tour data in the app
  • delete your account and associated data from the app's account screen
  • deny calendar or photo library permissions at any time in iOS Settings

Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as needed to provide TourBuddy and operate your account, unless a longer retention period is required by law or necessary for security, fraud prevention, or service integrity.

If you delete your account, we will take reasonable steps to delete or de-identify associated data, subject to technical and legal limitations.

Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect information we store and process. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children's Privacy

TourBuddy is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

International Users

If you use TourBuddy outside the country where our services are operated, your information may be processed in other countries where we or our service providers operate.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the last updated date on this page and may provide additional notice where required.
